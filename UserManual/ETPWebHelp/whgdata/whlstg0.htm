Agg Bid Qty

Aggregate Bid Quantity shows the total number of buying lots until that level. On  each row, Agg Bid Qty sums up the quantity for that order with all the quantities  displayed above that order so that the market participants can see the total no of  lots they can sell until that level

Ask Agg Qty

Ask Aggregate Quantity shows the total number of selling lots until that level. On  each row, Ask Agg Qty sums up the quantity for that order with all the quantities  displayed above that order so that the market participants can see the total no of  lots they can buy until that level.

Ask Price

The best price the seller is asking for the quantity displayed

Ask Qty

The number of selling lots of a single order

Bid Price

The best price at which the buyer is willing to pay for the quantity displayed.

Bid Qty

The number of buying lots a single order.

Buyer Time

The time when the Buyer submitted the buy order

Client

Is an individual or a group of individuals that have an agreement with a member for specific type of commodity trade.

Contract

A combinations of Grade + Warehouse + Production Year

MCP

Member client position

Non-STP

Non Straight Through Process, a client whose instruction needs to be approved by  respective representative.

Price-Time

This is an algorithm the matching engine uses to match trades, for buy order the best price will be the higher price given and for Sell order the best price is the least price given whereas the best time is the prior time for both cases.

Seller Time

The time when the Seller submitted the sell order

STP

Straight Through Process, a client type whose instruction can be taken as an order  without representatives approval.

Symbol

The symbol of the contract. For example the symbol WSDB3 indicates the Washed Sidama  Grade 3 coffee.

Warehouse

Warehouse code of the product. AW means Awassa, JM means Jimma, DD Dire Dawa, etc.

Year

Year of production for that specific Commodity (2006, 2007, 2008, etc.)
